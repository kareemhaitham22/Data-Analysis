Introduction¶

the data set selected contains patients showed and unshowed in their medical appointments, and we need to analyze the reasons and variables that make them not showing up at their medical appointments

Questions¶

1) Does WaitingDuration has a big influence on non showing up for appointments?

2) Does Age has a big influence on non showing up for appointments?

3) Does SMS has a big influence on non showing up for appointments?

4) Does a certain gender influence whether one shows up to appointments?

5) Does Scholarship influence whether one shows up to appointments?

6) Does Hipertension influence whether one shows up to appointments?

7) Does Diabetes influence whether one shows up to appointments?

8) Does Alcoholism influence whether one shows up to appointments?

9) Does Handcap influence whether one shows up to appointments?

def my_pie_chart(labels,cut_points,colors,title):
    fig1, ax = plt.subplots()
    ax.pie(cut_points, labels=labels, colors=colors, autopct='%1.1f%%')
    plt.title(title, fontsize= 20)
    ax.axis('equal')
    plt.show()
In [35]:
labels = 'No SMS_received, No-show', 'No SMS_received, Showed', 'SMS_received, No-show', 'SMS_received, Show'

colors = ['red', 'yellow', 'blue', 'green']
title = 'No-show vs SMS_received'
my_pie_chart(labels,cut_points,colors,title)

it is clear that the percentage recieved an sms and not showed up for thier medical appointments is more than the sum the percentages of all patients who showed up for thier medical appointments whether they recieved a SMS or not.

Therefore recieving SMS didn't encourage patients to show up for thier medical appointments

Conclusions¶

_As the waiting duration increases patients tends for not showing

_patients between 0-10 and 45-55 are the peak for non showing up

_recieving SMS didn't encourage patients to show up for thier medical appointments

_Females tends to not show up for thier medical appointments

_Scholarship encourage patients to show up for thier medical appointments

_patients not having Hipertension tends to not show up for thier medical appointments

_patients not having Diabetes tends to not show up for thier medical appointments

_patients not having Alcoholism tends to not show up for thier medical appointments

_patients not needing special needs tends to not show up for thier medical appointments

Limitations¶

_Most of the visualization part is limited to pie chart due to the plenty of categorical data type columns

_Statistical testing and more detailed data segmentations would give us much more better results.

_data is from 2016, therefore need to be updated

_getting data for patients around the world would give us much more accurate results than only limiting data to brazil patients
